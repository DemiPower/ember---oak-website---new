About Us

Welcome to Ember & Oak, a rustic steakhouse where bold flavors meet warm, hearty meals. We're passionate about sourcing high-quality ingredients and preparing them with precision, creating dishes that are both satisfying and memorable. Come join us today, where you are guaranteed to have a great meal, AND a great time.
